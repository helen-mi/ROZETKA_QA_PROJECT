Project: Rozetka QA Testing

Test Cases:

TC-01: Search product
Steps:
1. Open website
2. Enter product name in search
3. Press search
Expected result: list of products is shown

TC-02: Open product page
Steps:
1. Search product
2. Click on product
Expected result: product page opens

TC-03: Add product to cart
Steps:
1. Open product page
2. Click "Buy"
Expected result: product is added to cart

TC-04: Check cart
Steps:
1. Add product to cart
2. Open cart
Expected result: product is visible in cart

TC-05: Remove product from cart
Steps:
1. Open cart
2. Click remove
Expected result: cart is empty or updated
